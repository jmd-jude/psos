Different product strengths require fundamentally different stakeholder engagement strategies. Eg: Sales teams often pitch 'superior data quality' to CMOs when the real competitive advantage may be 'seamless integration' - which requires buy-in from engineering teams who never hear the pitch. The AI-Readiness dimension represents a new category that bridges technical implementation with business innovation strategy.
